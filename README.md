## 📖 About the Project / ಯೋಜನೆಯ ಬಗ್ಗೆ

**"ಭಗವದ್ಗೀತೆಯ ಬೆಳಕು" (The Light of Bhagavad Gita)** is an educational initiative aimed at introducing children and students to the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita. Rather than a traditional religious text, this project is structured as a **Moral Science and Life Skills manual**.

It features a curated collection of **150 essential Shlokas**, meticulously translated into simple, spoken Kannada and plain English. The goal is to help students navigate modern challenges like stress, lack of focus, and behavioral issues through ancient wisdom.

## ✨ Key Features / ಪ್ರಮುಖ ಲಕ್ಷಣಗಳು

* **Bilingual Translation:** Every shloka is presented in Sanskrit (Devanagari script), accompanied by simple Kannada and English meanings.
* **Student-Centric Themes:** The shlokas are categorized into practical themes:
    1.  **Karma Yoga (ಕರ್ಮಯೋಗ):** The Art of Work, Duty, and Focus.
    2.  **Mind Control (ಮನೋನಿಗ್ರಹ):** Discipline, Anger Management, and Mental Strength.
    3.  **Bhakti Yoga (ಭಕ್ತಿಯೋಗ):** Devotion, Simplicity, and Trust.
    4.  **Jnana Yoga (ಜ್ಞಾನಯೋಗ):** Knowledge, The Soul, and Overcoming Fear.
    5.  **Values & Good Habits (ಮೌಲ್ಯಗಳು):** Non-violence, Truth, and Cleanliness.
* **Teacher & Parent Friendly:** Translations are non-academic, making it incredibly easy for educators and parents to explain the concepts to children without needing Sanskrit expertise.
* **Audio/QR Integration:** Designed to pair with video/audio resources for accurate pronunciation (perfect for school assemblies).

## 📚 Content Structure Example / ಮಾದರಿ ಶ್ಲೋಕ

Each entry in this repository/book follows a highly readable format:

**ಸಂಸ್ಕೃತ (Sanskrit):** कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन |  
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि ||  

**ಶ್ಲೋಕ (Kannada Transliteration):** ಕರ್ಮಣ್ಯೇವಾಧಿಕಾರಸ್ತೇ ಮಾ ಫಲೇಷು ಕದಾಚನ |  
ಮಾ ಕರ್ಮಫಲಹೇತುರ್ಭೂಃ ಮಾ ತೇ ಸಂಗೋಸ್ತ್ವಕರ್ಮಣಿ ||  

**ಕನ್ನಡ ಭಾವಾರ್ಥ (Kannada Meaning):** ನಿನಗೆ ಕೆಲಸ ಮಾಡಲು ಮಾತ್ರ ಅಧಿಕಾರವಿದೆ, ಆದರೆ ಅದರ ಫಲಗಳ ಮೇಲೆ ಅಧಿಕಾರವಿಲ್ಲ. ಫಲದ ನಿರೀಕ್ಷೆಯಿಲ್ಲದೆ ಕರ್ಮ ಮಾಡು; ಹಾಗಂತ ಕರ್ಮ ಮಾಡುವುದನ್ನು ಬಿಡಬೇಡ.  

**English Meaning:** You have a right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions. Never consider yourself the cause of the results of your activities, nor be attached to not doing your duty.

## 🎯 Target Audience / ಯಾರಿಗಾಗಿ?

* **Schools & Educational Institutions:** As a supplementary curriculum for Moral Science or Value Education classes.
* **Teachers:** As a daily teaching aid for morning assemblies.
* **Parents:** For homeschooling or instilling cultural values and daily discipline at home.
* **Students (KG to 10th Std):** To build character, confidence, and concentration.
